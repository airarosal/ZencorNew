Our Mission

Our Vision

To be the most trusted partner for agriculture in the Philippines, known for good service and reliable products.

Our Story

Zencor started in 2015 right here in Pampanga with a simple goal: to help local farmers deal with their daily challenges in the field.

We saw how hard it was for farmers to fight pests and poor soil conditions. We wanted to offer solutions that actually work without harming the land they rely on. Today, we are proud to support our farming partners throughout the Philippines with the products they need for a healthy and bountiful harvest.
